About me:

I am Evangelia but you can call me Vanessa (it is sort of a nickname or a roepnaam as the Dutch people say). I am 34 years old, I come from Greece and I moved in the Netherlands 3 years ago. I live in the city of Utrecht with my husband and our dog, Lisa.
I studied Music Science and History, but my working background is in Retail Management. I decided to change career and become a software developer not only because of the great job opportunities out there, but also because of the huge rewards in terms of self development and creative expression. Last but not least I love the fact that as a software developer -every day- one is presented with new challenges, working on potentially very complex problems. Love it!
Even though I miss my country and its beauties, moving to the Netherlands was one of the best choices I've ever made. Good organization and infrastructure, diversity, safety is amongst the things I like the most. Plus one gotta love the completely unpredictable weather. HA! (I am only joking. I lived on an island where the weather was similar to the weather here. I had prior experience in "all four season" days.)
